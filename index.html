Lemontree Tennis Club

Come meet up! Play hard! Celebrate our love for tennis and our love for food every Saturday morning from 9am - 2pm!

One of the great benefits is that the club is also a social club! There is a bar and food available; and there is a nice buzz about the place! By Dave Smith
